OUR PROCESS

Mobile App Strategy

Strategy development is the very first step of mobile application development process. It involves discussing the basic idea to map out the development process.

Mobile App Design

Preparing the Mobile App Design is the next step of our process. The designers brainstorm to come up with the most relevant and efficient and tasteful designs for a particular idea, ensuring a great customer experience.

Mobile App Development

Mobile App development of the application body. The task is assigned to a team of developers most suited for the project. We stay connected with the clients to take iterations and keep them informed about the progress.

User Acquisition

We provide guidance and assistance to our clients, even after the application launch. We help them in strategizing for maximum user-acquisition. We provide analytics and other similar solutions to you, so that you application can reach its true potential.

Mobile App Analytics

Code-Brew Labs provides Mobile app analytics to ensure continuous great performance and steady growth of your whole project.

Lean & Agile Development

A Development Model Engineered at its Core For Startups and Fast Growth Companies.

We practice a powerful agile development framework we call ‘Rapid Agile’ combining best practice from Extreme app programming , Kanban, Lean, SCRUM and an intense yet rapid requirements gathering process. The results speak for themselves:

  • Wrap up work in lesser time than have been promised by other Mobile app developers.
  • Adopting ‘crisis’ projects and turning it over by budgeting and keeping to deadlines.
  • Working with the comprehensive outlook to input change any time during the project.

Our App development framework is disciplined enough to prevent ‘cowboy’ coding, yet flexible enough to allow mid-development change of direction.
